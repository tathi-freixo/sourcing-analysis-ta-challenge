AI-Generated Insights & Recommendations
✦ CLAUDE AI
🚨 Critical Bottleneck: Test → Offer
Only 30.8% of candidates who completed the technical test received an offer. This is the steepest drop in the funnel. Investigate whether the bar is too high, misaligned, or whether evaluators are inconsistent at this stage.
⚡ Github & Inbound: Prioritize These Channels
Github (11.0%) and Inbound (10.1%) deliver the highest hire rates overall. But channel ROI is role-dependent — Github is 26.7% for Analista de Recrutamento and 0% for TA Specialist. Match channel to role.
✅ Early Signal: Triple Score Rule
Candidates with Tech ≥80, Behavior ≥75, Manager ≥75 have a 17.9% hire rate — more than double the average. Implement this as a prioritization filter to focus recruiter time on high-probability profiles.
🔍 Recruiter Calibration Gap
Bruno closes 100% of his offers (15/15). Gustavo generates 11 offers but closes only 6. The issue may be offer quality, timing, or candidate experience. A calibration session between top and bottom performers could unlock significant gains.
📵 No Response = #1 Killer
210 candidates (58% of all rejections) simply never responded. Inbound and Banco de Talentos show the best response rates. Revisit outreach messaging for cold channels — a structured 2nd reach-out cadence could recover meaningful pipeline.
🎯 Bet on Senior+ & Hybrid — But Check the Role
Staff converts at 13.3% vs. Junior 4.6% globally. But Junior Analista de Recrutamento hits 18.5%. Hybrid outperforms Remote across all roles (worst: Analista de Dados Remote at 1.9%). Always combine seniority and work mode decisions with role context.
🗂️ Role Drives Channel Strategy
LinkedIn works for TA Specialist (15%) but fails for People BP (0%). Banco de Talentos is 21.1% for Analista de Recrutamento but 0% for Analista de Dados. A one-size-fits-all sourcing strategy wastes budget and recruiter time.
🔮 Next Level: Predictive Scoring by Role
With more data volume, a simple regression model could predict hire probability by combining role + channel + seniority + work mode — turning these dimensions from descriptive into prescriptive, telling recruiters where to focus before they source.
